Who We Are

Established in greater Atlanta in 2004, we built AmeriFreight on true American values such as integrity, innovation, and quality of service. It is not an accident our company colors mirror the U.S. flag: red, white, and blue.

The auto shipping industry can be complicated, and we strive to simplify it for our customers. Although we have shipped thousands of cars over the past two decades, we always consider the fact that many customers have never shipped a car before and deserve to know the entire process. AmeriFreight continues to bring honesty, accountability, and transparency into the complexities of the auto transport industry.

It is a core value to tailor every customer interaction around their unique situation and never use a one-size-fits-all approach. Customers at AmeriFreight find authenticity, not scripted telephone calls.

AmeriFreight operates with a 'people first' approach in everything we do. We aspire to create a great work environment for our team. We firmly believe that a happy and enthusiastic team will deliver excellent service to our customers.

We treat our customers like we treat our employees: like family. Beyond just shipping cars, we want to be a small part of your story. We are proud to have received thousands of customer reviews telling us that we are solidly on track. Each good review is the sound of applause.

Our team takes pride in the amazing work they do for customers every day. You will be in good hands when you choose AmeriFreight for your car shipping needs.

To learn more about why you should choose AmeriFreight, or to learn more about how the process works, please visit the pages below.

THE MOST IMPORTANT FEATURES OF AN AUTO TRANSPORT COMPANY

FEATURES AMERIFREIGHT IMPORTANCE
Discounts
 We provide a wide range of discounts including: Military, First Responders, Seniors, Students, Return Customers, and Early Bird.
Insurance
 We vet all the carriers and ensure they carry adequate insurance to cover your car during loading, unloading, and transport. This is standard for ALL transports at AmeriFreight at no additional cost.
AFTA GAP Protection
 A carrier's regular insurance may not protect your vehicle from all damage and inconvenience that can happen during shipment. AFTA GAP Protection offers additional protection over the carrier's standard insurance and to give you added peace of mind.
Customer Reviews
 Reviews are an excellent indicator of the type of service you can expect when shipping a car. Our 20-year track record speaks for itself. AmeriFreight has industry-leading reviews with the BBB, Google, and many others.
Upfront Payment
 We do not take upfront payments. We only accept payments after you approve a carrier that can meet your needs.
Carrier Vetting
 AmeriFreight vets all the carriers we present to you, and makes sure they are properly registered with the U.S. Department of Transportation, while also being properly insured. We only use highly-ranked carriers using both an internal and external rating system.
Experience Matter
 We have shipped every kind of vehicle to every state in the country. We are well-prepared for your shipping needs and are ready to serve you with confidence. AmeriFreight is proudly one of the oldest companies in the auto transport industry.
At AmeriFreight we believe in doing the right thing. Honesty, compassion, a desire to serve with enthusiasm, and do a good job fuel the work we do. And we do it well.
