Read These Tips to Find the Cheapest Way to Ship a Car

Need some tips on shipping a car inexpensively? Although the shipping cost varies based on the factors previously mentioned, there are several ways to lessen the cost. Check out the tips below and take advantage of as many as you can!

  • Use Open Transport: This is the car shipping industry's standard method of shipping cars. Cars are transported via an open-air tractor-trailer. Open carrier transport is the most affordable option.
  • Have Your Vehicle in Operable Condition: Double-check to see if your vehicle runs and drives before calling a car shipping company. It is more expensive for carriers to handle inoperable cars.
  • Schedule Early: If you reserve a pickup/drop-off time in advance, you can avoid expediting costs or last-minute requests that can tack on extra fees.
  • Ship During Low Season: It usually costs less to ship a car during the "low" fall and winter seasons than during the "high" spring and summer seasons.
  • Ship From a Major City: It is typically cheaper to ship a car to a more populated area. We recommend bringing your vehicle near a larger city if you live in a rural area.
  • Ship Multiple Cars if You Have Them: We offer a discount to customers who ship more than one vehicle on the same truck (see below for more discount offers).
  • Take Advantage of Our Discounts: We are proud to offer discounts to military members, first responders, students, senior citizens, Early Birds who book with us no later than 48 hours after receiving a quote, return customers, and those with multiple vehicles.

Seasonal Insights: How Summer and Winter Impact Car Shipping Rates

When planning your auto shipping, it’s crucial to consider how different seasons can affect both the car shipping cost and overall timing of your shipment. Whether you’re looking for car shipping quotes during peak summer months or the colder winter season, understanding these trends can help you plan ahead and potentially save money.

Summer Shipping Rates: High Demand, Competitive Pricing

Summer is the busiest time of year for car shipping. With schools out and many families relocating, demand for car transport quotes skyrockets. This increased competition can lead to slightly higher rates in some areas, but it can also bring more competitive pricing as shipping companies vie for customers. During this season, you may find that car shipping quotes fluctuate, especially if you're shipping across long distances or to popular destinations.

To keep costs down, it's recommended to book your shipment early, as last-minute requests may result in premium pricing. On average, summer shipping rates can rise by 10-15%, particularly for cross-country moves or enclosed transport services. Despite the higher demand, auto shipping costs can still vary significantly based on your location and the type of transport (open vs. enclosed).


Winter Shipping: Lower Demand, Potential Delays

In contrast, winter is generally a slower season for auto transport, which often leads to reduced rates. However, shipping a car in winter comes with its own set of challenges, such as inclement weather and shorter daylight hours. Snow and ice can delay deliveries, and routes to colder regions can be more difficult to navigate, which may cause slight increases in car shipping costs for those areas.

That said, if your schedule is flexible, winter can offer great opportunities to lock in lower car shipping quotes, especially if you are shipping to southern states where demand is lower during the colder months. For instance, shipping a car in January or February often leads to reduced rates, but it’s essential to plan ahead to accommodate potential weather delays.



When to Book for the Best Rates

For both summer and winter, booking your auto shipping in advance can make a significant difference in the final car shipping cost. By comparing car transport quotes from various providers, you can secure a competitive rate no matter the season.
